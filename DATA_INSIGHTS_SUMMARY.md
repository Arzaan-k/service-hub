Should be standardized to:
- **DAIKIN** (currently: DAIKIN, Daikin, DAikin)
- **CARRIER** (currently: CARRIER, Carrier, CARRIER-THINLINE)
- **THERMOKING** (currently: THERMOKING, Thermoking, TK-MP4000)

Should be:
- **40FT REEFER**

## KEY RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. Workload Distribution
- **SHAHBUDDIN is overloaded** with 35.7% of all jobs
- Consider redistributing work to balance technician utilization
- Train additional technicians to reduce dependency on top 3 technicians

### 2. Preventive Maintenance Focus
- 44% of services are PM - continue this proactive approach
- Target the high-maintenance containers (GESU9460634, BMOU9782197) for more frequent PM
- 5.3% refrigerant gas top-ups suggest leak prevention program needed

### 3. Client Relationship Management
- **Qwik Supply** (125 services) needs dedicated account manager
- Top 10 clients represent significant revenue - ensure SLA compliance
- Crystal (own company) has 67 services - internal fleet management needed

### 4. Data Standardization Required
- Implement dropdown validations for:
  - Machine Make (limit to 10-15 options)
  - Container Size (limit to 10 standard sizes)
  - Locations (city master list)
  - Used/New status (3 options only)
- Enforce uppercase/lowercase consistency

### 5. Inventory Management
- 38.4% of jobs require parts - maintain safety stock
- Common failure items: Display, Keypad, Contactors, Refrigerant
- 44% courier delivery suggests need for better inventory positioning

### 6. Temperature Control Focus
- Temperature issues are #1 complaint category
- Implement proactive temperature monitoring
- Schedule more frequent calibration for problematic units

### 7. Documentation Enhancement
- Add cost tracking fields (missing from current data)
- Add service duration (start/end times)
- Add GPS location verification
- Add customer satisfaction ratings
- Add warranty tracking
